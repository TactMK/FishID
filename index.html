1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

User Identifiers: When you use the App, we may assign a unique user ID. This ID helps us manage your subscriptions, maintain service continuity, and understand usage patterns without directly linking activities to your real identity.

Subscription and Purchase Information: If you subscribe to our premium services (including automatic weekly renewals), we collect information related to your subscription status and purchase history. This may include subscription type, duration, renewal status, transaction identifiers, and payment confirmation records from our subscription service provider, RevenueCat.

1.2 Usage Information

Analytics Data (Amplitude): We use Amplitude, a third-party analytics service, to understand how you interact with the App. Data includes screens viewed, buttons pressed, and duration of feature usage. We use this information solely to improve the App’s functionality, design, and performance.

Fish Image Analysis and Classification: You may capture images directly within the App or upload images from your device’s photo library for classification. These images are processed through AI models provided by OpenAI (using the “gpt-4o-mini” model) and facilitated by AIProxy.
Image Handling: Photos remain on your device unless you choose to classify them. When you request classification, the image is transmitted securely to the external service for processing.
Storage of Images: We do not permanently store your images on our servers. Images are used only as needed to process your request and then are discarded.
Non-Fish Images: If the submitted image does not contain a fish, the classification request returns an error. We do not store these non-fish images.

1.3 Device and Technical Information

Device Data: We may collect device model, operating system version, language settings, and unique identifiers.

Log Data: Our servers may automatically record certain information (“Log Data”) such as IP addresses, App version, request times/dates, and general usage patterns.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and Improve the App: Understand usage patterns to enhance features, reliability, and user experience.
  • Process Subscriptions and Purchases: Manage payment verification, subscription renewals, and account authentication via RevenueCat.
  • Analytics and Performance: Use Amplitude data to guide improvements.
  • Research and Development: Refine the fish identification process. Any temporarily stored data is anonymized or de-identified.
  • Customer Support: Assist with inquiries, troubleshoot problems, and provide technical support.

The primary purpose of using your data is to improve the App’s functionality and enhance the user experience.
